ADJUSTING ENTRIES

a.    Insurance for one month has expired.

                    Insurance Expsense                    20,000

                                              Prepaid Insurance                    20,000

b.    Medical Supplies on hand at month-end amounted to ₱21,000.

                    Medical Supplies Expense                     35,000 (56,000 - 21,000)

                                              Medical Supplies                                35,000

c.    Depreciation on the medical building and on the medical equipment is ₱5,000 and ₱9,000, respectively.

                    Depreciation Expense – Building                     5,000

                    Depreciation Expense – Equipment                9,000

                                            Accumulated Depreciation – Medical Building                     5,000

                                            Accumulated Depreciation – Medical Equipment                9,000

d.    Unearned research revenues in the amount of ₱30,000 have been earned..

                    Unearned Research Revenues                    30,000

                                              Research Revenues                                30,000

e.    Salaries of ₱51,000 have accrued.

                    Salaries Expsense                    51,000

                                              Salaries Payable                    51,000

e.    Interest on the 20% and 24% notes are ₱20,000 and ₱8,000, respectively.

                    Interest Expense                    28,000 (20,000 + 8,000)

                                              Interest Payable                     28,000
